**Comprehensive Summary of the Pizza Fusion Franchise Agreement**

This Franchise Agreement between Pizza Fusion Holding, Inc. (“Franchisor”) and the Franchisee governs the operation of a single Pizza Fusion restaurant under a proprietary system emphasizing organic, all-natural products and environmentally friendly practices. The agreement grants the Franchisee a limited, non-ownership license to use the Franchisor’s trademarks and system exclusively at a franchisor-approved location (“Premises”) within defined geographic areas for delivery, catering, and advertising.

**Term and Key Dates:**
- Initial term: 10 years from the Agreement Date.
- Renewal: Two additional 10-year terms available, subject to notice 9-12 months before expiration and compliance with renewal conditions.
- Opening Deadline: Franchise must open within nine months of the Agreement Date (default), with possible extensions only by written consent.
- Site approval: Franchisee must obtain site approval within 90 days and secure lease/purchase within 120 days of Agreement Date.
- Training: Mandatory initial training (up to 14 days total) before opening; failure to complete may lead to termination without extension of opening deadlines.
- Maintenance/refurbishment: Premises must be refurbished at least once every five years; required upgrades must be completed at least 60 days before term expiration.
- Marketing: Franchisee must spend at least 3% of gross sales quarterly on local marketing and contribute 3% (plus up to 4%) of gross revenues to franchisor-controlled Marketing and Regional Funds.
- Reporting: Monthly financial reports due within 20 days; annual reports within 90 days; customer lists quarterly.
- Audit rights: Franchisor may audit with 10 days’ notice; penalties apply for revenue understatement exceeding 2% on multiple occasions.

**Franchisee Obligations and Risks:**
- **Payments:**  
  - Initial franchise fee: $30,000 (non-refundable).  
  - Weekly royalties: 6% of gross revenues (excluding coupons, refunds, and taxes).  
  - Advertising contributions: 3% to Marketing Fund plus up to 4% to Regional Fund.  
  - Late payments incur 1.5% monthly interest plus escalating late fees ($100, $200, $300).  
  - Dishonored payments incur $100 fee per occurrence.  
  - Transfer fee on ownership changes: $7,500 or 25% of current initial franchise fee, whichever is greater.

- **Operational Controls:**  
  - Franchisee must operate only at approved Premises and within designated Delivery/Catering and Advertising Areas; no sublicensing or unauthorized sales channels.  
  - Must comply with franchisor’s quality, operational, branding, and product standards, including exclusive use of franchisor-approved suppliers and proprietary “Secret Recipe Products.”  
  - Must maintain required insurance coverages (e.g., $1M general liability per occurrence, $3M aggregate umbrella, workers’ compensation, fidelity bond).
